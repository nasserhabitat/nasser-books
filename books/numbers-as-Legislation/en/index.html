Book Description

This work presents an exceptional journey into the depth of Quranic numbers and human consciousness, transcending traditional interpretation to a symbolic reading in the jurisprudence of Quranic numbers, combining science and spirit, language and cosmos.

The book is not merely a numerical study, but an existential journey aiming to liberate numbers from their quantitative constraints, transforming reading from understanding quantity to understanding quality through the mirror of the Quran.

Methodological Vision

The book is based on a symbolic reading of numbers in the Quran, where each number becomes a window to consciousness, and every numeral a mirror of existence. It presents an integrated vision connecting discourse and balance, opening new doors in understanding the relationship between humans and the Quran through its numerical system.

The book combines deep numerical analysis with authentic spiritual dimension, presenting a comprehensive methodology for understanding the Quran as a mirror of self and cosmos through its numerical patterns.
