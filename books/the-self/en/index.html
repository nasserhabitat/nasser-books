Book Description

This work presents an extraordinary journey into the depths of the human being as described by the Quran, moving beyond traditional interpretation to a symbolic reading of Quranic linguistics, bridging science and spirit, language and cosmos.

The book is not merely a linguistic study, but an existential journey aimed at liberating the letter from its silence, transforming reading from understanding the text to understanding the self through the mirror of the Quran.

Methodological Vision

The book is based on a symbolic reading of the Quranic language, where each letter becomes a window to consciousness, and each word a mirror of existence. It presents an integrated vision connecting the self, heart, mind, and spirit, opening new doors in understanding the relationship between human beings and the Quran.

The book combines deep linguistic analysis with authentic spiritual dimensions, presenting a comprehensive methodology for understanding the Quran as a mirror of the self and the universe.

Ultimate Conclusion: From the Self to God

At the conclusion of this extraordinary journey, the book calls for the completion of consciousness and existence, where the human being merges with the universe in a single glorification. When the self understands its true nature, the barrier between the reader and the book disappears, between the human being and existence, between "He" and "I".

This book is an invitation to every reader to embark on a deep contemplative journey in the mirror of the Quran, to remember that everything in this existence is a word spoken in the beginning and then forgotten.
