Book Description

This book aims to reconstruct the proper understanding of religion and life by correcting prevailing concepts and presenting authentic perspectives on Quranic terminology, inspired by the essence of the Qur'anic language.

The methodology of this book is based on the theoretical foundations and methodological frameworks detailed in "The Jurisprudence of the Qur'anic Tongue: A New Approach to Understanding Text and Manuscript."

Comprehensive Vision of the Book

This work represents the comprehensive encyclopedia for applying the methodology of "Jurisprudence of the Qur'anic Tongue" in reconstructing Quranic concepts. The author presents in 417 pages a comprehensive journey through fundamental Quranic terminology, focusing on liberating it from inherited interpretations and returning it to its linguistic and Quranic origins.

The book is distinguished by presenting a practical methodology for interactive contemplation that enables the reader to build a living relationship with the Quranic text, going beyond superficial reading to deep understanding that connects Quranic concepts with their practical applications in contemporary life.

The Book's Objective

This work aims to enable the reader to build a living relationship with the Qur'an through deeper understanding of its purposes, applying its teachings as a comprehensive book of guidance in all aspects of life, and contributing to building a society that derives its values from divine revelation.

The book presents a comprehensive methodology for liberating Quranic terminology from human additions and interpretations that have obscured the original meanings over centuries, and restores the Quran's status as the original source and fundamental reference for understanding religion and applying it in contemporary life.
