Book Description

This book aims to reconstruct the proper understanding of religion and life by correcting prevailing concepts and presenting authentic perspectives on Quranic terminology, inspired by the essence of the Qur'anic language.

The methodology of this book is based on the theoretical foundations and methodological frameworks detailed in "The Jurisprudence of the Qur'anic Tongue: A New Approach to Understanding Text and Manuscript."

Key Features of the Book:

  • The methodology of "Interactive Contemplation" that goes beyond superficial reading
  • Precise linguistic analysis of Quranic terminology
  • Utilization of modern technology as supportive tools
  • Comprehensive presentation that transcends traditional problematic approaches
  • Practical applications for daily life

Specialized Article Series:

Faith and Creed Concepts:

  • Deconstructing the concept of "Divinity and Lordship"
  • The relationship between God and Gabriel
  • Attributes of believers as practical skills

Precise Quranic Concepts:

  • Spiritual purification and refinement
  • Remembrance with its spiritual and psychological dimensions

Reunderstanding Worship:

  • Prayer as a journey of awareness and transformation
  • Pilgrimage as a cognitive journey
  • Fasting as a methodology for contemplation

Correcting Misconceptions:

  • Concepts of killing, coercion and false deities
  • The reality of abrogation in the Qur'an
  • Jinn and devils between reality and superstition

The Book's Objective

This work aims to enable the reader to build a living relationship with the Qur'an through deeper understanding of its purposes, applying its teachings as a comprehensive book of guidance in all aspects of life, and contributing to building a society that derives its values from divine revelation.
