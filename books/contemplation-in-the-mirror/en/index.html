Book Description

Contemplation in the Mirror is a foundational work presenting a new methodological framework for interacting with the Qur'an. It introduces a comprehensive vision and a modern theoretical framework for contemplation that transcends traditional, fragmented approaches to interpretation and memorization.

The author argues that true contemplation is an active, dynamic process—a 'journey of interactive contemplation'—that allows the reader to engage with the text and discover its profound meanings as if seeing their own reflection in a mirror. This method aims to restore the Qur'an's role as a practical guide for every aspect of modern life, moving it from a theoretical object of study to a living source of wisdom and guidance.

It integrates traditional Islamic scholarship with modern digital tools, focusing on the Ottoman script and its significance in understanding the Qur'anic text, offering practical examples for contemplation.

Methodological Vision

The book adopts the concept of "Fiqh al-Sab' al-Mathani" (Jurisprudence of the Seven Oft-Repeated) as a foundation for understanding the Qur'an's linguistic structure. It provides a modern framework for analyzing Qur'anic manuscripts using digital tools, ensuring accessibility and precision in contemplation.

It bridges the gap between classical Islamic scholarship and contemporary technology, offering practical applications for readers to engage with the Qur'an in a meaningful and transformative way.

Key Features of the Book:

  • Presents a modern, integrated methodology for contemplating the Qur'an.
  • Links contemplation to practical life and social development.
  • Emphasizes the Qur'an's role as a comprehensive book of guidance.
  • Utilizes modern digital tools to enhance the contemplation process.
  • Discusses the challenges and opportunities of contemporary contemplation.

The Book's Objective

The core objective of Contemplation in the Mirror is to empower the reader to rediscover the Qur'an as a dynamic, relevant text for their lives. It seeks to establish a new foundation for interacting with the divine word, transforming a passive reading experience into an active journey of self-reflection and intellectual and spiritual growth, enabling the reader to derive actionable wisdom and guidance for personal and social progress.

Conclusion: Contemplation as a Way of Life

This book aims to revive contemplation as a way of life, connecting the Muslim community with the Qur'an through a blend of traditional scholarship and modern digital tools. It invites readers to embark on a journey of interactive contemplation, making the Qur'an a living source of guidance in the modern era.

This work is a call for every reader to integrate contemplation into their daily lives, drawing from both heritage and contemporary tools to achieve a profound and meaningful understanding of the Qur'an.

Who Is This Book For?

  • Quranic Studies Researchers: Those interested in advanced methodologies for Qur'anic contemplation
  • Technology Enthusiasts: Individuals keen on applying digital tools to Islamic studies
  • Linguists: Those exploring the linguistic structure of the Qur'anic text
  • Educators and Preachers: Those seeking modern approaches to teaching the Qur'an
  • General Readers: Anyone seeking a deeper connection with the Qur'an
