Book Description

This book presents an in-depth study of the Seven Mathani (Oft-Repeated Verses) in light of the linguistic and numerical structure of the Quran, proposing a new vision that unites geometry and meaning in understanding the Quranic text.

The book is not merely a traditional linguistic or numerical study, but an exploratory journey aiming to reveal the single cosmic geometric law that governs the entire Quranic text, where the concept of "Seven Mathani" becomes not just a label for Surah Al-Fatihah or the long chapters, but the single cosmic geometric law that governs the entire Quranic revelation.

Methodological Vision

The book is based on an integrated reading of the Quran that combines deep linguistic structure with precise numerical arrangement, revealing the dual-layered "Mathani" system that connects every letter, every chapter, and every stage of human history.

The book combines precise linguistic analysis with meticulous numerical study, presenting a comprehensive methodology for understanding the Quran as a book of geometry that connects creation, scripture, and human consciousness.
