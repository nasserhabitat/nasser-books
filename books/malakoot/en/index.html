Book Description

This book aims to rebuild the foundation of correct understanding of religion and life by correcting prevalent concepts and presenting original insights into Qur'anic terminology, inspired by the essence of the Qur'anic language.

The methodology of this work is based on the theoretical foundations and methodological principles detailed in the book "Jurisprudence of the Qur'anic Language: A New Approach to Understanding Text and Manuscript".

Key Features

  • Explores the linguistic intricacies of Qur'anic terminology
  • Provides a methodological framework for understanding Qur'anic texts
  • Corrects misconceptions in traditional interpretations
  • Integrates digital tools for textual analysis
  • Applicable to both scholars and general readers

Book Description

This encyclopedic work presents a comprehensive vision of the universe from a Qur'anic perspective, combining deep scientific analysis and authentic spiritual understanding of cosmic verses, with systematic criticism of contemporary astronomical theories.

The book is not merely a scientific study but a faith journey aiming to transform belief into certainty through contemplation of God's signs in the horizons and within oneself.

Key Features

  • Comprehensive analysis of Qur'anic cosmic verses
  • Scientific critique of modern astronomical theories
  • Exploration of the Earth's centrality in the Qur'anic worldview
  • Integration of material and spiritual dimensions
  • Evidence-based arguments for the Earth's stability

Book Description

This foundational work presents a new methodological framework for interacting with the Qur'an, introducing a comprehensive vision for contemplation that transcends traditional approaches to interpretation and memorization.

It integrates traditional Islamic scholarship with modern digital tools, focusing on the Ottoman script and its significance in understanding the Qur'anic text, offering practical examples for contemplation.

Key Features

  • Modern methodology for Qur'anic contemplation
  • Links contemplation to practical life and social development
  • Emphasizes the Qur'an's role as a comprehensive guide
  • Utilizes digital tools to enhance contemplation
  • Addresses challenges and opportunities in contemporary contemplation
