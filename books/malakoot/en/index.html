Book Description

This encyclopedic work presents a comprehensive vision of the universe from a Quranic perspective, combining deep scientific analysis and authentic spiritual understanding of cosmic verses, with systematic criticism of contemporary astronomical theories.

The book is not merely a scientific study but a faith journey aiming to transform belief into certainty through contemplation of God's signs in the horizons and within oneself.

Key Features:

  • Comprehensive study of the universe as presented in the Holy Quran
  • Systematic scientific critique of mainstream astronomical theories
  • In-depth analysis of cosmic verses about heavens and earth
  • Integrated vision combining material and spiritual dimensions
  • Quranic and scientific evidence for Earth's stability and centrality

Main Themes:

  • Quranic Perspective of the Universe: Heavens, earth and what is between them
  • Nature of Earth: Between Quranic evidence and scientific theories
  • Sun-Moon System: Analytical study of cosmic verses
  • The Throne and The Chair: Material and symbolic dimensions in the Quran
  • Critique of Pseudoscience: Analysis of modern astronomy fallacies
