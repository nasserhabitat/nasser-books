Book Description

This work presents an exceptional journey into the depth of Quranic language and human consciousness, moving beyond traditional interpretation to a symbolic reading of the jurisprudence of Quranic language, combining science and spirit, language and cosmos.

The book is not merely a linguistic study, but an existential journey aimed at liberating the letter from its silence, and transforming reading from understanding the text to understanding the self through the mirror of the Quran.

Methodological Vision

The book relies on a symbolic reading of the language of the Quran, where each letter becomes a window to consciousness, and every word a mirror of existence. It presents an integrated vision connecting the soul, spirit, and expression, opening new doors in understanding the relationship between humanity and the Quran.

The book combines deep linguistic analysis with authentic spiritual dimension, presenting a comprehensive methodology for understanding the Quran as a mirror of the self and the cosmos.

The Great Conclusion: From the Soul to God

At the conclusion of this exceptional journey, the book calls for the completion of consciousness and existence, where humanity merges with the cosmos in a single glorification. When the soul understands its truth, the barrier between the reader and the book, between humanity and existence, between "He" and "I" disappears.

This book is an invitation to every reader to embark on a deep journey of reflection in the mirror of the Quran, to remember that everything in this existence is a word spoken in the beginning and then forgotten.
