Book Description

This work presents an exceptional journey into the concept of Spirit in the Quran, Divine Data, and the relationship between Self, Spirit and Heart. It transcends traditional interpretation to offer a symbolic reading in the jurisprudence of Quranic language, combining science and spirit, language and cosmos.

The book is not merely a linguistic study, but an existential journey aimed at liberating the letter from its silence, and transforming reading from understanding the text to understanding the self through the mirror of the Quran.

Methodological Vision

The book is based on a symbolic reading of the Quranic language, where each letter becomes a window to consciousness, and each word a mirror of existence. It presents an integrated vision that connects between self, spirit and expression, opening new doors in understanding the relationship between human beings and the Quran.

The book combines deep linguistic analysis with authentic spiritual dimension, presenting a comprehensive methodology for understanding the Quran as a mirror of self and cosmos.

Ultimate Conclusion: From Self to God

At the conclusion of this exceptional journey, the book calls for the completion of consciousness and existence, where the human being merges with the cosmos in a single glorification. When the self understands its true nature, the barrier between reader and book disappears, between human and existence, between "He" and "I".

This book is an invitation to every reader to embark on a deep journey of contemplation in the mirror of the Quran, to remember that everything in this existence is a word that was spoken in the beginning and then forgotten.
