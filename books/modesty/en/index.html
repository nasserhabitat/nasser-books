About This Book

This groundbreaking work attempts to transform our understanding of "modesty" from a static moral concept to a dynamic cognitive tool for Quranic comprehension. Dr. Nasser Ibn Dawood presents a revolutionary approach that reveals modesty as both a spiritual compass and an intellectual methodology.

Through Quranic and historical analysis, the book examines the manifestations of modesty in Quranic texts and the Prophetic biography, focusing on its role in building individuals and society, and confronting ethical challenges in the digital age.

Methodological Vision

The book adopts an analytical methodology combining Quranic exegesis and ethical studies, presenting modesty as a cognitive tool that aids in deeply understanding Quranic texts. It connects modesty as a spiritual quality to its role as a key to contemplation, offering a civilizational vision for its application in contemporary life.

Key Features:

  • Presents a new vision for modesty as a cognitive tool for understanding the Quran
  • Connects ethics with Quranic contemplation
  • Examines the manifestations of modesty in the Quran and Sunnah
  • Addresses the challenges of modesty in the digital age
  • Establishes the role of modesty in building Islamic society
