About This Book

This groundbreaking study presents a new methodological approach to understanding Allah's Most Beautiful Names, moving beyond traditional narratives to a functional and analytical perspective rooted exclusively in the Qur'anic text. The work employs a strict tawqif methodology, recognizing only those divine names explicitly mentioned in the Holy Qur'an while rejecting commonly cited names without Qur'anic foundation.

Key Features of the Study:

  • Identifies 89 original names confirmed in the Qur'an while rejecting 40 non-tawqifi names
  • Develops a "bridge to functional names" framework for practical application
  • Analyzes divine name conjugations and their significance in understanding cosmic management
  • Provides detailed examination of each name's meanings, psychological impacts, and manifestations
  • Offers practical guidance for applying this knowledge in daily spiritual practice

Book Structure:

  1. Part I: Concept and Foundations - Establishes the theoretical framework and methodology
  2. Part II: Functional Names Analysis - Examines name categories, groups, and conjugations
  3. Part III: Meanings and Dimensions - Detailed study of each confirmed divine name
  4. Part IV: Practical Applications - Guidance for implementing this knowledge in daily life
