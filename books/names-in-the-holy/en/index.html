About This Book

This groundbreaking study presents a new methodological approach to understanding Allah's Most Beautiful Names, moving beyond traditional narratives to a functional and analytical perspective rooted exclusively in the Qur'anic text. The work employs a strict tawqif methodology, recognizing only those divine names explicitly mentioned in the Holy Qur'an while rejecting commonly cited names without Qur'anic foundation.

The book aims to delve into the understanding of these names from a "functional" perspective, tracing their practical and applied connotations in their Quranic contexts. It explores how Allah's laws in managing the universe and the affairs of creation are manifested through them, and how these names can be invoked and activated in supplication, reliance on Allah, problem-solving, and dealing with life's challenges to strengthen certainty, remove anxiety, and achieve happiness and tranquility. The book seeks to answer a fundamental question: "How do we apply our understanding of the names in our daily lives?"

Key Sections of the Book:

  1. Part I: Concept and Foundations - Establishes the theoretical framework and adopted methodology.
  2. Part II: Functional Divine Names - Analyzes the functions of the names in their Quranic contexts.
  3. Part III: Meanings and Fruits of Allah's Beautiful Names - An analytical study of each name.
  4. Part IV: Practical Applications - Transforming knowledge into daily practice.

Methodological Vision

The book adopts a strict tawqif methodology, recognizing only those divine names explicitly mentioned in the Holy Qur'an and rejecting commonly cited names without Quranic foundation. It provides a functional analysis of the names, demonstrating how Allah's laws in managing the universe are manifested through them, along with practical applications to enhance certainty and achieve tranquility.

Conclusion: Allah's Beautiful Names as a Way of Life

The book aims to revive the understanding of Allah's Beautiful Names as functional and cognitive values, presenting a comprehensive vision of their role in enhancing Quranic contemplation and building society.
