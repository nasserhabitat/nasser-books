Book Description

This book presents the third and final part of the human journey in the mirror of the Quran, where we move beyond the text to the cosmos, and the entire existence becomes an open book that speaks with the voice of God.

The book is not merely a study of the physical universe, but an existential journey aimed at liberating consciousness from the limits of matter, and transforming the observation of the universe from watching objects to understanding meanings and implications.

Methodological Vision

The book is based on a symbolic reading of the universe as articulated by the Quran, where every being becomes a window to consciousness, and every creature becomes a mirror of truth. It presents an integrated vision that connects the self, the spirit, and the universe, opening new doors in understanding the relationship between humanity and existence.

The book combines deep scientific analysis with authentic spiritual dimension, presenting a comprehensive methodology for understanding the universe as a mirror of the self and the Divine.

Key Features:

  • A journey into the depth of the universe and human existence
  • Symbolic reading of beings in the Holy Quran
  • Union between science and spirit, matter and meaning
  • Analysis of the relationship between self, spirit and universe
  • Comprehensive vision for understanding the universe as a mirror of the self and the Divine

Main Themes:

  • When Beings Speak: From the static name to the living attribute
  • New Interpretive Perspective: On the verses about the creation of the heavens and the earth
  • Completion of the Circle: From the self to God through the universe
  • The Universe as a Living Book: All beings speak with the voice of God
  • Cosmic Reflection: From matter to meaning
